What is a Website

A set of related web pages located under a single domain name, typically produced by a single person or organization.

What are Websites

A website is a collection of webpages and related content that is identified by a common domain name and published on at least one web server. All publicly accessible websites collectively constitute the World Wide Web.

Examples of Websites

Examples of notable websites are Google, Facebook, Amazon, and Wikipidia.
